About us

Capt. john Chandra Mohan, a seasoned Master Mariner, embarked on a new voyage of entrepreneurship on February 28, 2022, with the inception of BOSA (Blue Ocean Sports Arena). Situated in the vibrant locale of Suriya Nagar, along Alagar Kovil Main Road, in Madurai, BOSA offers an array of sporting delights for enthusiasts of all ages. From badminton and football on pristine turfs to cricket matches filled with excitement, and even opportunities for archery and Silambam mastery, BOSA caters to diverse sporting interests.

Not only does it provide top-notch facilities such as ample car parking, CCTV surveillance for safety, and refreshing refreshments, but it also ensures the comfort of its patrons with well-equipped changing rooms and toilet facilities. Additionally, the turf benefits from a cutting-edge fog system, ensuring optimal playing conditions even in the warmest weather. With a commitment to nurturing young talent through coaching programs and engaging summer camps, BOSA stands as a beacon of sportsmanship and camaraderie in the heart of Madurai.
